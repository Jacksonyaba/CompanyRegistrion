The African Development Bank Group in its Macroeconomic Performance and Outlook for Africa,

indicated that the continent has seen positive growth despite multiple economic shocks in 2022, with

projections indicating stability for 2023 and 2024.

African nations projected to see significant growth in 2023-2024, with top-performing economies before Rwanda,

Côte d’Ivoire, Benin, Ethiopia and Tanzania, expected to surpass an average of 5.5% growth, reclaiming their spot among the world’s fastest-growing economies.
